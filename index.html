Research & interests

How I like to think

I am curious about methods that respect the messiness of human behaviour and still produce clear, actionable insights.

Areas I’m drawn towards

  • Time-series & sensor data – especially from wearables and real-world devices.
  • Experiment design & causal reasoning around pricing, interventions and behaviour.
  • Responsible & inclusive AI – how models interact with people at the margins.

Thinking style

I like to structure ambiguous problems into clear questions, identify what can and cannot be measured, and then choose simple, robust methods before fancy ones.

Most of my decisions start with a small scratchpad: assumptions, constraints, people involved, and failure modes. Only then do I reach for tools, models, or slides.
